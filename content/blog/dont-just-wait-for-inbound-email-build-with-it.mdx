## The Future Is Bidirectional

Email isn’t just output — it’s input.  
And input should be **as easy as output.**

That means:

- Instant webhooks for incoming mail
- Reliable delivery with retries + deduplication
- Attachments and metadata handled automatically
- Easy domain + MX setup that “just works”

That’s the future the Resend inbound beta is promising — and it’s the same future we’ve been building toward at inbound.new.
